How design & content requests flow

🗓️
Planned by default. Requests tied to a release go on a shared timeline with the dev sprint schedule — no surprises, nothing cut for time.
⚡
Ad-hoc lane exists. Content materials sometimes can’t wait for a planning cycle — flag them as ad-hoc and we triage together in this sync. Exception, not the default.
🤝
This sync is the venue. Planned items get slotted, ad-hoc items get triaged, and the timeline above stays current.
❓ Question for the group: what lead time does the team realistically need for release-tied content (e.g., the v1.5 launch campaign)? That number becomes the anchor for the request timeline.
Why v1.1 before v1.5: Apple required account deletion + upsell changes. Rather than risk the big v1.5 bundle in review, we ship a small compliant update that still opens the Society door — members get access now instead of waiting.
