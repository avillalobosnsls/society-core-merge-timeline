Merge phases — how we get there

The same releases as the overview, laid out as phases: the goal of each, what ships, the sequence of steps, and the gates that decide when it moves.

Overview Phases Technical
✅ Live on both stores

Phase 1 — Foundation & the new face · v1

June → Google Play July 12 · Apple App Store July 23

Goal: one NSLS account everywhere, and the members experience rebuilt on it — everything FOL/Core offers, with a new face and look.

What shipped

  • Centralized login live across Core, Society, and the app — Done
  • Session continuity — crossing between Core and Society keeps you signed in — Done
  • All of FOL/Core, carried over in full, with the new face and look — Live on Google Play (Android), July 12
  • iOS app live on the Apple App Store — Live, July 23

Why it mattered

  • Every later phase assumes one identity — without it, every doorway would mean a second login

Scope note

  • No Society content in this phase — deliberately; the doorways come next
⏳ In flight

Phase 2 — v1.1 · the doorways

Live as soon as approved by the App Store

Goal: open the door between Core and Society — both ways — without waiting for the big build.

Sequence

  1. Account deletion built and released — Done
  2. Submitted to Apple — July 20 — Done
  3. Society card + back-to-Core button build (Dev) — In flight, this week
  4. Apple App Store review — In flight, waiting on Apple
  5. Release on approval — Next

Outstanding — not yet started

  • Final CTA copy sign-off (Marketing) — targeted for this sync

Gates

  • App Store review — external, timing is Apple's
  • CTA copy locked before the card build ships

What ships

  • Society entry point on the Members Area dashboard — desktop and app
  • Button back to Core — no dead ends
  • No in-app product purchases — purchases happen on nsls.org
🎯 Targeting mid-August

Phase 3 — v1.5 · Society moves in

The big release

Goal: Society stops being a place you visit and becomes part of the app — tracks, onboarding, and journey-aware doorways everywhere.

Sequence

  1. Target reset to mid-August — Done
  2. Tracks + onboarding build (Dev) — In flight

Outstanding — not yet started

  • Entry-point banner copy per page (Marketing) — the first deadline
  • Entry-point banners on Orientation / LTD / SNTs / post-induction (Dev)
  • Track intro/outro + onboarding copy (Marketing)
  • Launch campaign (Marketing) — timeline locks at this sync

Gates

  • Content readiness — banners, track copy, campaign (lead time is the question for the group)
  • App Store review for the app release
  • Technical to-defines that land here: access visibility and admin access (see the Technical page)

What ships

  • Personal Insights → Career Clarity → Career Readiness, fully in-app
  • New welcome + onboarding, updated brand colors
  • Customized Society banners across the Members Area journey pages
Future

Phase 4 — v2 · no seams

Not yet scheduled

Goal: everything Society offers built natively into the app — no lines between Core and Society at all.

What it takes

  • Society exposes a native API for its content
  • App rebuilds Society surfaces natively on that API
  • Metrics & reporting and masquerade land with this phase — they come with the native behavior

When it gets real

  • Scope and timing picked up after v1.5 lands
Reading it: each phase gates the next — Phase 1 laid the foundation and the new face, v1.1 opens the doors, v1.5 moves Society in, v2 removes the seams. Owner: Angelica Evans (Product). Updated Jul 23, 2026.
